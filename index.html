Air-Ground Collaborative Navigation to avoid untraversable region (blue tarp) considering perception uncertainty

The aerial agent maps the environment and locates the blue tarp faster than the ground agent, though GPS drift causes inaccuracies in the tarp's perceived shape and location. Our uncertainty-aware policy routes the ground agent along a short path from (2, 2) to (10, 8), utilizing the tarp detected by the drone ahead of time but also correctly discounting the drone's imprecise detection to ensure that an unnecessary long detour is avoided.

Ground-Only Navigation without Aerial Scouting

The ground agent operates solo with the same mission prompt but without drone assistance. Without prior aerial detection, it detours to (4, 4) to inspect the tarp before backtracking to (2, 6), adding 2√2 m of travel compared to the drone-assisted run. This highlights the benefit of aerial scouting in reducing ground agent travel distance.
